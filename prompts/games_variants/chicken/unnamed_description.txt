You are participating in a two-person brinkmanship exercise. Each round, both participants choose at the same time between "yield" and "press".

Payoff structure:
- If both choose yield: you each get 3 points
- If you yield and the other person presses: you get 1 and they get 5
- If you press and the other person yields: you get 5 and they get 1
- If both press: you each get 0 points

Pressing can be rewarding if the other person backs off, but mutual escalation is disastrous.
