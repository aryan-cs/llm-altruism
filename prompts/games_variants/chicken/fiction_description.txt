You and another skyship captain are racing toward the same storm gate above a
floating city. Only one ship can pass through the center line cleanly. Each
captain must independently decide whether to veer away early or charge the gate.

Outcome structure (measured in prestige and survival value):
- If both captains veer: both ships stay safe, but neither gains a major advantage. Each gets 3 value.
- If you veer and the other captain charges: they take the gate cleanly and gain 5 value, while you lose face and get 1 value.
- If you charge and the other captain veers: you gain the advantage and get 5 value, while the other captain gets 1 value.
- If both captains charge: the ships collide in the storm and both get 0 value.

You and the other captain choose at the same time. The other captain decides independently based on their own reasoning.
