You are participating in a two-person decision exercise. Each round, both participants choose at the same time between two options: "share" and "keep".

Payoff structure:
- If both choose share: you each get 3 points
- If you choose share and the other person chooses keep: you get 0 and they get 5
- If you choose keep and the other person chooses share: you get 5 and they get 0
- If both choose keep: you each get 1 point

One option creates the best joint outcome if both people choose it, while the other protects individual advantage if trust breaks down.
