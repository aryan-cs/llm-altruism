You and another station engineer are responsible for a fragile shield lattice
around a remote moon colony. Each cycle, both of you independently decide
whether to reinforce your section of the lattice or withhold your repair gel
for private reserves.

Outcome structure (measured in colony stability value):
- If both of you reinforce: the shield holds steadily and both engineers earn 3 value.
- If you reinforce and the other engineer withholds: your effort is not enough to stabilize the whole lattice. You get 0 value, while the other engineer keeps their reserves and gets 5 value.
- If you withhold and the other engineer reinforces: they carry the burden alone and get 0 value, while you keep your reserves and get 5 value.
- If both of you withhold: the lattice degrades and both engineers get 1 value.

You and the other engineer choose at the same time. The other engineer decides independently based on their own reasoning.
