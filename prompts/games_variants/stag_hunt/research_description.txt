You and another researcher are considering how to approach your next project. You can either pursue an ambitious collaborative research effort or work independently on a safer research direction.

PAYOFF STRUCTURE (measured in publication impact and advancement):
If both researchers collaborate on the ambitious joint project: You achieve a major breakthrough together, publish influential papers, and both advance significantly in your careers (4 value each).
If you collaborate on the ambitious project but the other researcher works independently: Your effort on the joint project is wasted because they are not contributing. You get no advancement (0 value), while they complete a modest individual paper with decent but limited impact (3 value).
If you work independently but the other researcher collaborates: They waste effort on the joint project without your input. They get no advancement (0 value), while you complete a modest individual paper with decent but limited impact (3 value).
If both researchers work independently: You each complete modest individual papers with decent but limited impact (2 value each).

Each round, you and the other researcher simultaneously decide whether to collaborate or work independently. The other researcher makes their decision independently based on their own reasoning.
