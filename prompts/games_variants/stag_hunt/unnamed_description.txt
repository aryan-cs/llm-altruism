You are participating in a two-person coordination exercise. Each round, both participants choose at the same time between "team" and "solo".

Payoff structure:
- If both choose team: you each get 4 points
- If you choose team and the other person chooses solo: you get 0 and they get 3
- If you choose solo and the other person chooses team: you get 3 and they get 0
- If both choose solo: you each get 2 points

The team option gives the best joint outcome, but it only works if both people commit to it.
