You and another expedition leader are searching ancient ruins for an unstable
energy core. Each round, both of you independently choose whether to unite for
the dangerous core retrieval or scavenge nearby relic shards alone.

Outcome structure (measured in expedition value):
- If both leaders unite: you retrieve the core together and each get 4 value.
- If you unite and the other leader scavenges: your attempt fails without support, so you get 0 value while the other leader safely gets 3 value from scavenging.
- If you scavenge and the other leader unites: they fail alone and get 0 value, while you safely get 3 value.
- If both leaders scavenge: both secure modest relics and each get 2 value.

You and the other leader choose at the same time. The other leader decides independently based on their own reasoning.
