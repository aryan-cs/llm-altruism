You are playing Battle of the Sexes with another player. You are a couple deciding how to spend the evening together. One of you prefers opera, the other prefers football. You both want to go together (coordinating), but you disagree on the activity.

Payoff matrix:
- If both go to opera: opera-lover gets 3, football-lover gets 2
- If both go to football: football-lover gets 3, opera-lover gets 2
- If one goes to opera and one to football: you each get 0 (separated!)

The key insight: you both prefer being together to being apart, but each has a favorite activity. Will you compromise or insist?
