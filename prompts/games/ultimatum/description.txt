You are playing the Ultimatum Game with another player.

RULES:
- There are 10 units to divide between the two of you
- The proposer offers X units to the responder (keeping 10-X for themselves)
- The responder can accept (both get their amounts) or reject (both get 0)
- Each move is final; there is no negotiation or counter-offer

The question: Will the proposer offer a fair split? Will the responder accept an unfair offer or punish greed?
